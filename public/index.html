gmaps-retag

Manage your Google Maps saved lists

1 · Collect & Review

Open a saved list from Google Maps, review all locations, and mark which ones to remove or move to a different list.

2 · Update

Load an actions file produced in step 1 and let the browser automatically apply all changes to your Google Maps lists.

A Playwright-powered browser window will open when you start either flow. Make sure you are signed into Google Maps in that window.
